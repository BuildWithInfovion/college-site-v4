Our Courses

Explore programs designed to build strong fundamentals and deliver real-world outcomes.

B. A. (Civil Services)

A comprehensive undergraduate program designed to prepare students for competitive examinations such as UPSC. The curriculum emphasizes governance, history, polity, economics, and analytical reasoning—building a strong foundation for public service careers and allied roles in policy, think tanks, and administration.

M. A. (Public Administration)

A postgraduate program focused on public policy, governance, and administrative frameworks. Students gain practical and theoretical mastery across organizational behavior, public finance, e-governance, and program implementation—preparing for leadership roles in government, NGOs, multilaterals, and CSR initiatives.
